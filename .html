Introduction to Volcanoes

Volcanoes are fascinating natural formations that result from the Earth's internal heat and activity. They often have a cone-shaped appearance and can erupt with lava, ash, and gases.

Types of Volcanoes

  • Stratovolcanoes
  • Shield Volcanoes
  • Cinder Cone Volcanoes
